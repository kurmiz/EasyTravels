Make the Most of Your Evening

NIGHTLIFE TIPS

Follow these tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience while exploring Bhairahawa's nightlife.

Transportation

Use reliable transportation services like taxis or book through our transport service. Avoid walking alone in unfamiliar areas late at night.

Carry Local Currency

Many smaller establishments may not accept credit cards. Carry sufficient Nepalese Rupees for your evening activities.

Opening Hours

Most nightlife venues in Bhairahawa operate between 6:00 PM and 12:00 AM. Some restaurants and cafes may close earlier.

Emergency Contacts

Save important phone numbers: Police (100), Tourist Police (01-4247041), and our 24/7 tourist helpline (071-520111).
