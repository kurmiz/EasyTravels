Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Lumbini from Bhairahawa?

Lumbini is approximately 27 kilometers (about 17 miles) from Bhairahawa. The journey typically takes around 45 minutes by car or taxi. Bus services are also available and take about an hour.

What are the operating hours for transportation?

Most transportation services in Bhairahawa operate from 6:00 am to 10:00 pm daily. Some services may be available 24/7 with advance booking, especially for airport transfers.

How many days should I plan for visiting Lumbini?

We recommend allocating at least 3-4 days to properly explore Lumbini and surrounding attractions. This gives you enough time to visit the Maya Devi Temple, Ashoka Pillar, meditation parks, the various international monasteries, and other nearby attractions like Lumbini Aqua Park, Lumbini Cable Car, Siddha Baba Mandir, Daunne, and Nuwakot.

What other attractions are near Lumbini?

Besides the main Lumbini complex, nearby attractions include Lumbini Aqua Park (water park), Lumbini Cable Car (scenic ride), Siddha Baba Mandir (temple), Daunne (hill station), and Nuwakot (historical site). These can be visited as day trips from Bhairahawa.
